About GPTInsights

The objective of this academic project is to instruct individuals in the use of ChatGPT through valuable insights, providing a deep understanding of artificial intelligence technology.

The approach is implemented through a dedicated website, entirely in English, serving as a platform to convey educational content about ChatGPT. The focus is on empowering viewers to explore and make the most of this tool, promoting interaction and active participation. The project aims not only to provide technical information but also to create an engaging and educational experience.
